New report on AI inequalities at work for TUC Cymru

by

Data Justice Lab

|

May 9, 2025

The Data Justice Lab has produced a new

report

[in English]

report

[in Welsh] commissioned by the Trade Union Congress Cymru reviewing research on AI inequalities at work. The report, authored by Lina Dencik, Jessica Brand, Philippa Metcalfe and Cate Correia Hopkins, draws on studies from across the world to review how AI impacts five categories of workers: Age, Women, Disability, Ethnicity, and Minority Language Speakers. The report will be publicly launched soon.
