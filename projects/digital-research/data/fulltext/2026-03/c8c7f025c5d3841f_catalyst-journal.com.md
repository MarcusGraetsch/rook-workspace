Socialist revolutions have all but disap-peared during the past half century, even if they remain a remote possibility in authoritarian countries with corrupt, long-standing autocrats. By contrast, the achievements of broad social and democratic rights have rendered insurrectionary politics a strategic cul-de-sac in advanced capitalist countries. The era of revolutions is likely over.

The Left has always had a special interest in revolutions, at least since the time of Karl Marx.
