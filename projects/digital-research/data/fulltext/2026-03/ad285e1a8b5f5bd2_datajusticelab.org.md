A new website for the Data Justice Lab

by

Data Justice Lab

|

Nov 21, 2025

We are finally switching to our new website! We hope it will be clearer and easier to navigate. At the same time, we have tried to keep the main features and designs of the old site (which has been running since the start of the Lab in 2017). The goal is still to provide clear and straightforward information about the work of the Data Justice Lab and to make available our reports, publications, conference announcements, etc etc, in a simple way.

Some content on the site is still being added and updated, and some links may not yet work properly. In this transitional period, there might still be a few glitches here and there. But we’re working on it. We hope you like the new site!
