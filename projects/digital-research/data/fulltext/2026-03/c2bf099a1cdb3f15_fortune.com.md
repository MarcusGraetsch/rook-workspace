The commercial real estate legend received applause for his comments this week, but then, he was speaking to a friendly audience. NYU’s Schack Institute of Real Estate hosted the luncheon as part of its annual REIT Symposium, with real estate execs and many grad students hoping to reach Zell’s level of success in attendance.
