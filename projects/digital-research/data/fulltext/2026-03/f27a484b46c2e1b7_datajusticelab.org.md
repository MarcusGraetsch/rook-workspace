New project on Democracy, AI and Big Tech

by

KaOS Tecnologia Consciente

|

Aug 9, 2024

We are delighted to have received funding to pursue a new project starting in September that will explore corporate activities in the advancement of artificial intelligence and their implications for trust, governance, and democracy. The 2-year project is part of the Trans-Atlantic Platform scheme and is a collaboration between Lab co-directors Lina (Goldsmiths, University of London, UK) and Joanna (Western University, Canada) and Professor Victor Pickard at the Media, Inequality and Change Center at Pennsylvania University, US. Post-doc opportunities for the project will be announced later in the year.
