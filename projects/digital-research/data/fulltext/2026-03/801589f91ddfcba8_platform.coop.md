About the series:

Artificial intelligence is increasingly discussed in the cooperative sector, often without a clear understanding of how these systems work or what their adoption entails. This event series provides concise, accessible education to support more informed decision-making around AI in cooperatives.

Designed for cooperative leaders and students across sectors, the sessions move beyond hype to examine AI through a cooperative lens, with attention to governance, ownership, labor, and values-aligned use. The series combines a clear, structured discourse with space for continued dialogue and sector-wide learning, supporting responsible and strategic engagement with AI across the cooperative movement.
