Lab completes workshop series with public sector professionals

by

KaOS Tecnologia Consciente

|

Aug 9, 2024

On 18 July, we completed an 8-month series of meetings with public sector professionals on how to engage citizens with policy- and decision-making about the roll-out of data and AI systems in the public sector. Led by Arne Hintz with support from our partner organisation

Action Learning Associates

, the meetings brought together data and engagement officers from local authorities in England and Wales to discuss challenges and opportunities for involving citizens in data-related debates and decisions. We will review and publish some of the results later this year.
