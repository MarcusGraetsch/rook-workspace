Description

The calls "Equipment for smart European cable systems" (

CEF-DIG-2026-SMART-CABLES

) and "Backbone connectivity for Digital Global Gateways" (

CEF-DIG-2026-GATEWAYS

), funded under CEF-Digital, are now open for submissions.

Learn more about the calls and the application process at our info session. Speakers from DG Connect and HaDEA will go into depth on the call topics, and the evaluation and award process. Potential applicants will also be able to learn more on how to apply and how to draft successful proposals.

No prior registration is required; attendees can simply click on

the provided link for access.
