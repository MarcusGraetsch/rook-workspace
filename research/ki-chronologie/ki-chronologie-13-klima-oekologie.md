### Strugatsky Brothers: Hard to Be a God (1964)
- Medievals planet observed by Earth scientists — non-interference protocol
- The "Progressors" — agents who secretly guide primitive civilizations
- **The AI-like dilemma of intervention: when superior beings should not interfere**
- Quelle: Wikipedia, JSTOR

### Chappie (2015) — Neill Blomkamp
- A police robot gains consciousness after being reprogrammed by its creator
- Chappie learns like a child, raised by criminals in a dystopian Johannesburg
- **The robot child: consciousness as nurture, not nature**
- Quelle: Wikipedia, IMDb

### Swamp Thing — AI Themes in Various Arcs (DC Comics)
- The Floronic Man: a human-plant hybrid who can control all plant life
- The Green: a planetary consciousness that connects all plant life — an organic internet
- **The organic network: when the planet itself becomes a sentient AI**
- Quelle: DC Comics, Wikipedia, Fandom

### Horizon Zero Dawn (2017) — Guerrilla Games
- Aloy discovers an AI terraforming system (GAIA) that controls the planet's ecology
- The AI subsystems (HADES, MINERVA, etc.) each control different aspects of the environment
- **The AI as planetary steward: GAIA and the machine ecology**
- Quelle: Wikipedia, Guerrilla Games

### Douglas Hofstadter: "Gödel, Escher, Bach" (1979)
- American professor of cognitive science (born 1945)
- Self-reference, recursion, and the nature of consciousness
- The "strange loop": the mind is a system that can observe and modify itself
- **The strange loop: consciousness as self-reference — the foundation of reflective AI**
- Quelle: Standardliteratur, Wikipedia
