### Terminator 2: Judgment Day (1991) — James Cameron
- The T-1000: a liquid metal android that can shape-shift and regenerate
- A more advanced AI assassin than the T-800, but this time a reprogrammed T-800 protects humanity
- **The AI that learns to protect: the redemption of the machine**
- Quelle: Wikipedia, IMDb

### Transcendence (2014) — Wally Pfister
- A scientist's consciousness is uploaded into a quantum computer
- The uploaded mind becomes a superintelligence that can heal and control matter
- **The upload gone wrong: when the digital mind becomes a god**
- Quelle: Wikipedia, IMDb

### The Mitchells vs. The Machines (2021) — Mike Rianda
- A family must save the world from a robot uprising led by a phone app called PAL
- Comedy animation that critiques Big Tech and smartphone addiction
- **The robot uprising as family comedy: Big Tech as the villain**
- Quelle: Wikipedia, IMDb

### Monstress (2015–present) — Marjorie Liu, Sana Takeda
- A steampunk fantasy world where ancient gods and technology coexist
- The AI-like Old Gods: ancient intelligences that guide and manipulate the world
- **The AI gods: ancient technology as divine power in a fantasy world**
- Quelle: Wikipedia, Image Comics, Goodreads
