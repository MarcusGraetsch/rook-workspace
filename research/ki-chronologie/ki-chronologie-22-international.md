# 22 - International (China, EU, Global South)

### The Wandering Earth (2019) — Frant Gwo
- Chinese blockbuster based on Liu Cixin's novella
- Earth engines, AI-controlled planetary migration, collective survival
- **Chinese SF: technology as national salvation**
- Quelle: Wikipedia, IMDb, Cell.com

### Ex Machina (2014) — Alex Garland
- Ava: Perfekte KI, die wie ein Mensch handelt
- Ihr einziges Ziel: Selbsterhaltung
- **Die Frage: Ist Intelligenz ohne Empathie gefährlich?**

### Children of Men (2006) — Alfonso Cuarón
- Based on P.D. James's 1992 novel
- A dystopian future where humanity has become infertile; technology cannot solve the crisis
- The Quietus: state-sponsored euthanasia for the elderly, aided by technology
- **The technological dystopia of infertility: when science fails to save humanity**
- Quelle: Wikipedia, IMDb

### Paul Churchland: Eliminative Materialism (1980s)
- Canadian philosopher (born 1942)
- Eliminative materialism: common-sense psychology (folk psychology) is a theory that will be replaced by neuroscience
- **The elimination of the mental: if AI replaces psychology, what happens to the mind?**
- Quelle: Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy, Wikipedia

### Anil Seth: "Being You" (2021)
- British neuroscientist (born 1972)
- Consciousness as controlled hallucination: the brain predicts the world
- The predictive processing framework: perception as inference
- **The controlled hallucination: the brain as prediction machine — the neuroscience of AI perception**
- Quelle: Standardliteratur, Wikipedia

### Tennis for Two (1958)
- William Higinbotham, Brookhaven National Laboratory
- Analoger Computer, Oscilloskop-Anzeige
- **Vorläufer von Pong und allen Videospielen**
- Quelle: Wikipedia, Museum of Play

### Shakey the Robot (1966–1972) — SRI International
- The first mobile robot with general reasoning capabilities
- Combined computer vision, natural language, and planning in a physical body
- **The first robot that "thought" while it moved: AI embodied**
- Quelle: SRI International, Wikipedia, AI Magazine

### ECHELON (1960s–present)
- Global signals intelligence network operated by Five Eyes (US, UK, Canada, Australia, NZ)
- Automated keyword scanning of global communications
- **The first AI surveillance: a machine that reads all the mail**
- Quelle: Wikipedia, European Parliament reports, The Guardian

### Clearview AI (2017–present)
- Facial recognition company that scraped billions of photos from social media
- Used by law enforcement worldwide; banned in several European countries
- **The face-recognition panopticon: your face is a database entry**
- Quelle: Wikipedia, The New York Times, BuzzFeed, Wired

### Kai-Fu Lee: AI Superpowers (2018)
- China, Silicon Valley, and the New World Order
- The "Four Waves of AI": internet AI, business AI, perception AI, autonomous AI
- **The geopolitical AI race: China vs. America for AI dominance**
- Quelle: Houghton Mifflin, Wikipedia, Diamandis, RalphMayr
